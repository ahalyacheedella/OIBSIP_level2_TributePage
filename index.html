Sushant Singh Rajput

Sushant, in full Sushant singh rajput, (born January 21, 1986, Patna, Bihar, India—died June 14, 2020, Bandra West,Mumbai), Indian actor known for his work in hindi cinema and he appeared in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list twice since 2017.

Career

  • 2008: his debut show was the romantic drama Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil.In 2011, a biopic on the life of the Indian cricket team captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni was conceived by Dhoni's manager, Arun Pandey.
  • 2009-2011: followed by the lead role in the soap opera Pavitra Rishta.In the meantime in March 2017, Rajput was signed in by the director Abhishek Kapoor and the producer Ekta Kapoor for their film called Kedarnath.
